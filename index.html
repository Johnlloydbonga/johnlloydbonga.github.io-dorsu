ABOUT


ORIENTAL STATE COLLEGE OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY is an educational institution established and sustained by quality education. It seeks to successfully implement and improve its Quality Management System (QMS) in order to better serve its students and the community, further contributing to nation building.


VISION

A university of excellence, innovation and inclusion


MISSION

To elevate knowledge generation, utilization and distribution.

To promote inclusive sustainable development through research-based higher quality education, technical-vocational skills, responsive to the needs of local and global community; and

To produce holistic, creative, and inclusive human resource who are responsive and resilient to global challenges while maintaining strong sense of nationhood.

REGISTRAR


UNIVERSITY REGISTRAR


The Office of the University Registrar is a repository of records. It serves as the frontline service unit that stewards the academic records of students and handles curricular and academic information, student’s admissions. Registration, grades verification, evaluation, accreditation, graduation, production of diplomas and transcript of records. Responsible for monitoring the integrity and authenticity of each record. We make them available upon request but subject to the existing rules and regulations.

Student’s Records are kept in perpetual and considered classified as confidential and unclassified or non-confidential records. Furthermore, this office counsels’ students, staff & faculty on academic matters to enforce the policies and regulations of this University.


Servicess Offered


Issuance of Transcript of Records (TOR);

Issuance of Diploma

Issuance of Certification, Authentication and Verification

Issuance of General Weighted Average (GWA)

Issuance of Certifications: Earned units; PD 907(Automatic C.S Eligibility); Registration, Report of Grades, Honorable Dismissal, etc.

Preparation, Submission and Distribution of Academic Calendar.

Assist on Registration and Enrollment Matters.

Verify student scholastic records and performance.

Updating of deficiency grades.

Admission Requirements

UNDERGRADUATE


INCOMING FIRST-YEAR STUDENTS

  • Admissions Profile Form
  • Form 138-A with original signature of the School Principal
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • PSA-authenticated Birth Certificate
  • Certified Photocopy of Marriage Contract (for married women only)
  • PSA-authenticated Birth Certificate of Husband (for married women only)
  • 5pcs. recent 2×2 ID picture
  • 1 short brown envelope
  • 1 long brown envelope
  • 1 window envelope with mailing stamp

RETURNING STUDENTS

  • Admissions Profile Form
  • University Clearance
  • Report of Grades (ROG) for the last semester attended
  • 1 window envelope with mailing stamp

TRANSFEREES

  • Admissions Profile Form
  • Informative Copy of the Transcript of Records (TOR)
  • Certificate of Transfer Credential or Honorable Dismissal
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • PSA-authenticated Birth Certificate
  • 5pcs. recent 2×2 ID picture
  • 1 short brown envelope
  • 1 long brown envelope
  • 1 window envelope with mailing stamp
